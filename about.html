I´m Radka

With a background in Finance, Marketing and Management studies, I have started on a new journey learning web development. I’m passionate about web technologies with a focus on front-end engineering and UX/UI design.

COVID-19 has impacted many people across the globe, including me. When lockdown was announced back in 2020, I had no clue what was going to happen with my job. Our industry has been widely affected and I was asking myself what the best next step is. As I browsed the internet I read an article which sectors are most stable and not threatened by this turbulent time. IT sector was a clear choice.

I was motivated to start something new. I did a little research on the knowledge I need to learn for the successful transit of my career. Out of curiosity I searched on Czechitas website, which is an organization focused on IT talents looking for a new job in the IT field. Shortly after, I took a html, css course and I started coding as a way to gain a new skill during the long days of lockdown.
